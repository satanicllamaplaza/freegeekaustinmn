Make A Donation

If you would like to donate your unused computers or computer accesories please follow the detailed instructions below.

Send an email to: DONATE@FREEGEEKSOUTHERMN.ORG

Please include the following information in your email.

  • Your Name
  • Your Phone Number
  • Availability
  • Pickup Location
  • List of Items for Donation

We will only take items from the list below

  • Laptops
  • Computer Towers
  • Harddrives
  • RAM
  • Computer Monitors
  • Computer Accesories

These items do not need to be functional. Any damaged goods will be repaired or scrapped for parts.

After you email us, one of our team members will reach out and schedule a pickup.
